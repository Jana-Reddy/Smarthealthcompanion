First Aid Guide

Essential first aid tips to help you quickly respond to common emergencies.

Severe Bleeding
  • Apply firm, direct pressure to the wound using a clean cloth.
  • If possible, elevate the injured area above heart level.
  • If bleeding does not stop, seek emergency assistance.
Burns
  • Hold the burned area under cool running water for 10-20 minutes.
  • Do not apply ice or oily substances to the burn.
  • Cover with a sterile gauze bandage.
Choking
  • Ask the person to cough forcefully if they can.
  • If unable to breathe or speak, perform the Heimlich maneuver.
  • Call emergency services if the object does not come out.
Fainting
  • Lay the person flat and elevate their legs.
  • Loosen tight clothing and improve fresh air flow.
  • Seek medical help if consciousness is not regained quickly.
